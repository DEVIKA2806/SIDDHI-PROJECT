OUR STORY

We are a mother-and-daughter team with a simple dream: to share a little bit of peace and warmth from our home to yours. Everything you see here is handpoured with love, care, and an attention to detail that only small batches can offer. We believe the best candles are made not in big factories, but with simple, honest work right on our floor.

Our inspiration comes directly from the rich traditions and warmth of an Indian home. We pour our candles using techniques passed down and perfected over time, blending time-honored craft with the finest natural ingredients. Our promise is simple: we create more than just scents; we craft moments of tranquility. Thank you for supporting our small, family-run business and letting us share our passion with you.
